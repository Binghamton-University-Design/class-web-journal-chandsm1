Project 1: Typographic Riddles

For the shape of water I decided to mold the type into the typical water 'droplet' shape. At first I tried to morph the letters a set font, but soon realized that it would be more efficient to draw the letters into the shape I wanted using the pen tool.

Project 1: Typographic Riddles

I chose a few movie titles from the brainstorm to mock up. The same treatment was used with the words toy, express, and water, in that the letters were drawn with pen tool to fit the shape of an object (a book, a pole, and a droplet.)

Project 1: Final Riddle Book

We sifted through all of the mockups and chose the most effective ones to move forward into the book. We took into account color contrast in deciding the order of pages, along with other variables. The final 'Riddle Me This' online book is linked under my bio!

Project 2: Working With the Past

I brainstormed about possibilities of what I could turn into a pixel icon. I decided on an avocado, since I eat one almost every morning. After sketching it out, I moved my idea to digital using photoshop.
